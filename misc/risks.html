MIT scholars Andrew McAfee and Erik Brynjolfsson explain this in the book “Race Against the Machine”: The advances in computing technology included in Google’s smart cars represent the next wave of new technologies that will reduce job opportunities for the whole of society. This would render the value of many skills once considered very secure.
